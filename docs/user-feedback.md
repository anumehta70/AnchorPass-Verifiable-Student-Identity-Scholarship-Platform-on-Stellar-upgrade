## Onboarding Instructions (send to classmates)

1. Install the [Freighter wallet](https://freighter.app) browser extension.
2. Open Freighter → switch network to **Testnet**.
3. Copy your wallet address from Freighter.
4. Visit **https://anchorpass.vercel.app** (replace with your live URL).
5. Click **Connect Wallet** and approve in Freighter.
6. Click **For Students → Student Dashboard**.
7. Claim the demo scholarship assigned to your wallet.
8. View your issued credential and screenshot the verification page.
9. Submit the feedback form inside the dashboard.
10. Send your name, wallet address, tx hash, and screenshot to the organizer.
